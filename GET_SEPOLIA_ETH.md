# Get Sepolia ETH - Quick Guide

## Step-by-Step:

1. **Copy your MetaMask account address:**
   - Open MetaMask
   - Click on your account name (top)
   - Click "Copy address to clipboard"
   - You'll get something like: `0x1234...5678`

2. **Get Sepolia ETH from one of these faucets:**
   - **Alchemy Faucet**: https://sepoliafaucet.com/ (recommended)
   - **Infura Faucet**: https://www.infura.io/faucet/sepolia
   - **PoW Faucet**: https://sepolia-faucet.pk910.de/

3. **Paste your address** and request ETH (usually 0.1-0.5 ETH)

4. **Wait 1-2 minutes** for the transaction to complete

5. **Check your MetaMask** - you should see Sepolia ETH balance

---

**Note:** Some faucets require:
- Social media login (Twitter, Discord)
- Daily limits (0.1-0.5 ETH per day)
